About Us

At 1964 the institute was established at the premises of department of industries and commerce at industrial estate. Then the building was constructed and inaugurated by Thiru.R.Venkatraman formal minister of labour of industries in 1965.

Our institute stands majestically on Vellore Road, Vengikkal, Thiruvannamalai . which easily accessible from Railway Station(5km) and Central Bus Stand (4km).Near District Collector Office.

Mission

To develop an education system that caters the demand of the dynamic and liberalized world along with forming a chain to motivate and develop the entrepreneurial qualities among the candidates. It aims at producing them at a global level.

Vision

To develop an education system that caters the demand of the dynamic and liberalized world along with forming a chain to motivate and develop the entrepreneurial qualities among the candidates. It aims at producing them at a global level.
