Admission Details

Notification will be hoist in the Department website www.skilltraining.tn.gov.in in the month of May/June every Year. The list of ITI’s and Trades and other Criterion will be provided according to norms time to time. Anyone can upload their details on this website for getting Admission.

The candidates for admission are selected according to merit and communal rotation every year through District Wise Counseling. District Counseling is conducted by the Nodal Government ITIs located in each District. The candidates according to their rank will choose the course or ITI as per his choice. The selection order issued to each candidate on the spot.

The candidates thus allotted by the Department in the month of July every year will be trained in the concerned Institute as per National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT) norms of training.

Free Training to the Trainees of Government ITI
To encourage admission and active participation in skill training programme and to help the poor and downtrodden youth, tuition fee is waived by the Government and free training is offered to all the trainees of Government ITIs.
Freebies
The Government have sanctioned the following concessions to the Government ITI and Government aided Private ITI trainees:-

  1. Bus pass between their place of residence and the institute for 12 months
  2. Two sets of uniforms per year to all the trainees
  3. Bi-Cycle to all the trainees
  4. Laptop to all the trainees and
  5. Text Books and drawing instruments to all the trainees.
  6. One Set Shoe to all the trainees.
Stipend
Monthly stipend @ Rs.750/- to all the trainees irrespective of community and income
