Welcome To Govt. ITI, Thiruvannamalai

At 1964 our institute was established at the premises of department of industries and commerce at industrial estate. Then the building was constructed and inaugurated by Thiru.R.Venkatraman formal minister of labour of industries in 1965.

Our institute stands majestically on Vellore Road, Vengikkal, Thiruvannamalai . Which easily accessible from Railway Station(5km) and Central Bus Stand (4km) and Near District Collector Office.

Courses Offered

We provide training that is tailored to your career aspirations, and offer pathways that help you reach your potential. We pride ourselves on personalizing your learning experience to your individual needs and expectations, while supporting you to develop the skill set you need to succeed.

Principal’s Message

“கை தொழில் ஒன்றை கற்றுக்கொள் கவலை உணக்கில்லை ஒத்துகொள்.”

The college ensures student’s emotional growth flow with intellectual excellence. This empowers them to develop their self esteem, self awareness & confidence. Sharing ideas, analyzing situations & expressing them confidently are essential skills which are owned here, through experimental learning.

Technical education is the back bone of every nation and is the stepping stone for a country to move in the niche of a developed nation. We are in the mission of transforming rural India into a developed place by creating a skilled work force who would contribute to the growth and development of our nation. It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to the creative world of quality training as per the craftsman training scheme. Empowering the nation by empowering the youth is our motto. The main focus of our Institution is to empower our students with excellent training and make them suitable for the highly competitive global Industrial market. I wish all the best to all my trainees. We believe that success is inevitable where there exist firm determination, hard work and discipline. We assure that you will be proud of yourself as a confident and successful skilled worker after your training from this Institute. Once again I wish all my students a successful and rewarding career.

Govt.ITI, Thiruvannamalai is an amalgamation of competent teachers, state of the art infrastructure and an experienced & efficient administration. The college came in to existence with the vision of creating a safe & supportive environment for its students to provide a perfect balance of academics, sports, artistic & social opportunities. Youth learning should be a joy & we strive for that.

- Thiru. K. Ravi. D.A.E., Principal.
